Welcome to

B-Arcade Mall

Nairobi, Kilimani.

Located in the heart of Kilimani off Argwings Kodhek road, B-Arcade will attract shoppers in Kilimani and surrounding areas such as Kilimani, Lavington and Ngong road area.

B-Arcade will boast of a competitive mix of clients to ensure shoppers get a variety of items to do/shop while visiting.

With ample parking, B-arcade's target shoppers are the upper middle class who appreciate quality in their lifestyle and choice of products.
